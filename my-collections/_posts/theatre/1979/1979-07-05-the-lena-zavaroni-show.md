**Performances by Lena Zavaroni include:**
* A Tribute To The Young Talent Of Yesteryear
* I Dance All Night
* I Can't Stop Dancing
* I've Got The Music In Me
* Ma! He's Making Eyes At Me
* Bright Eyes
* Alexanders Ragtime Band
