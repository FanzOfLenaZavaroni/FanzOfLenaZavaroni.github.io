From the The Royal Albert Hall, London a special musical concert in conjunction with BBC Radio, to mark the centenary of the Music Publishers Association.

Lena sang a medley of Les Reed hit songs.

### Programme
* Joan Savage
* Julian Lloyd Webber
* Mike Sammes Singers
* Bucks Fizz
* Marti Webb
* Mike Sammes Singers
* Frankie Vaughan
* Anne Shelton

### INTERVAL
* Lena Zavaroni
* Hubert Gregg
* Mike Sammes Singers
* Semprini
* Vince Hill
* Acker Bilk
* Vera Lynn
