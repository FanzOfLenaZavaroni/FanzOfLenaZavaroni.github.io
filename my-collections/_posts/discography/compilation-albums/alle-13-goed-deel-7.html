Alle 13 Goed! Deel 7

1974

Alle 13 Goed! Deel 7 is part 7 of a large number of albums released under the title Alle 13 Goed! (en: All 13 Good!) released by Philips in the Netherlands.

Tracks

  1. Demis Roussos: My Only Fascination
  2. Liesbeth List: Te Veel Te Vaak
  3. Lena Zavaroni: Ma! (He's Making Eyes At Me)
  4. Love Unlimited Orchestra: Love's Theme
  5. Bonnie St. Claire And Unit Gloria: Voulez Vous
  6. Philip Goodhand-Tait: Leon
  7. Ciska Peters: España Mañana
  8. Mouth & MacNeal: Ik Zie Een Ster
  9. Vicky Leandros: Theo Wir Fahr'n Nach Lodz
  10. Rob de Nijs: He', Speelman
  11. Barry White: Never, Never Gonna Give You Up
  12. Sandra & Andres: True Love
  13. Paper Lace: Billy - Don't Be A Hero
    • The band won Opportunity Knocks for five consecutive weeks in 1973
