--- layout: television title: Lena description: image: /assets/images/BBC/ image-caption: --- During the opening credits Toast Marster Bryn Williams introduces Lena Zavaroni. Lena opened the show singing Sweet Sweet Smile & The Magic Is You, she then introduces The Nolans who sing Don't Make Waves. Lena and her dance troupe then perform a song and dance routine (But I was Cool), Lena then sings Even Now after which she introduces Les Dawson he performs a comedy Sketch with Lena. Lena thanks her Guests and ends the show performing a song and dance routine (Jump Down Jimmy).
